Add to Home Screen

In Safari, tap the Share button ↑ at the bottom of the screen, then tap Add to Home Screen. After that it launches like a native app and remembers your credentials.

API Credentials

Get your keys from developer.ecoflow.com — takes about a week to get approved. Keys are saved in your browser only.

Your Devices

Serial numbers are in the EcoFlow app → device → settings. Extra battery data is retrieved automatically — no EB serial numbers needed.
